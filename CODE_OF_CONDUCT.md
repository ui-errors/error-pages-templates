# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge
We as contributors and maintainers pledge to make participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Our Standards
Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:
- Being respectful and inclusive
- Accepting constructive criticism
- Focusing on what is best for the project

Unacceptable behavior:
- Harassment or discrimination
- Personal attacks
- Offensive comments

## Enforcement
Instances of abusive behavior may be reported to the project maintainers. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated.
